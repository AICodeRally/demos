POS Features

Showroom Catalog

20 items, category filters, SPIFF badges, "Add to Cart" tap. Products show live commission impact.

Rewards Panel

Tier staircase, live commission calc (SWIC engine), active SPIFFs, today's earnings, threshold progress.

AI Upsell Suggestions

Purple AI cards appear as items are added. Context-aware: bundle opportunities, protection attach, premium upgrade.

D365 Commerce Integration

Close Sale generates D365 TransactionEvent. Event log shows real schema. BroadcastChannel pushes to Manager.

Manager ↔ POS Interaction Flow

This is the "wow moment" in the pitch:

  1. Manager sees Sarah's attach rate is low (red traffic light)
  2. Clicks her card → sees AI coaching suggestion (purple): "Suggest bundle pitch"
  3. Clicks "Push to iPad" → BroadcastChannel sends coaching to POS
  4. POS Terminal (in the other tab) shows a toast: "Coaching from Manager: Focus on bundle pitch"
  5. Rep adds items → AI upsell card appears suggesting Adjustable Base
  6. Rep clicks Rewards tab → sees live commission update with bundle bonus
  7. Rep closes sale → D365 event fires → Manager feed updates in real-time
